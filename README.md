**Open Ecommerce** is a product-ready platform that helps ecommerce startups generate paid ads, website creatives, and email content that align with their unique **Brand DNA**.

Inspired by Google’s Pomelli, Open Ecommerce is designed to be simpler, more accessible, and tailored specifically for ecommerce use cases.

---

## 🚀 Why Open Ecommerce?

- **Tailored for Ecommerce**  
  Built specifically for ecommerce startups to generate creatives for paid ads, social media, websites, and email marketing.

- **Aligned with Brand DNA**  
  Automatically scans your website to extract your “Brand DNA,” ensuring all generated content stays consistent with your brand identity.

- **Easy to Use**  
  Generate high-quality creatives in just a few clicks using templates that adapt to your Brand DNA—no design expertise required.

- **Flexible AI Models**  
  Supports top models like GPT Image 2, SeaDream 4.0, and other leading AI tools—not limited to a single provider.

---

## ✨ How It Works

1. **Extract Brand DNA**  
   Analyze your website using Vision AI and headless browsing to understand your brand identity, visual style, and messaging.

   ![Extract Business DNA](https://anoreatdtemjsoyrjlnk.supabase.co/storage/v1/object/public/github/Extract%20Business%20DNA.gif)

2. **Select a Template**  
   Choose from templates for ads, social media, websites, emails, and more.

3. **Input Your Product**  
   Upload your product image, tagline, and optional reference images.

4. **Refine Outputs**  
   Review and fine-tune generated creatives to match your exact needs.
